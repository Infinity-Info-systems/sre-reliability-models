# SRE Maturity Scorecard

## Purpose

Measure how mature the reliability practice is across services and teams.

## Score Areas

- SLO adoption
- incident learning
- error budget governance
- service ownership
- reliability trend management
- incident learning quality

## Use

Use this scorecard to summarize how mature the reliability practice is across services and teams.

## Example Scale

- 1 = weak
- 2 = emerging
- 3 = defined
- 4 = managed
- 5 = strong
