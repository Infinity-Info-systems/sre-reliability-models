# Availability Model

## Purpose

The availability model describes how service uptime is measured and governed.

## Considerations

- service criticality
- measurement window
- dependency impact
- customer-facing versus internal service views
- service tiering

## Use

- define consistent reporting
- distinguish availability from resilience
- keep measurement rules explicit

## Interpretation

Availability should be measured in a way that reflects the user experience and the real business criticality of the service.
