# SLO/SLI Governance

## Purpose

SLO/SLI governance ensures teams define meaningful targets and review them consistently.

## Governance Practices

- define targets for critical services
- review SLOs on a recurring cadence
- tie alerts to service outcomes
- track breaches and error budget consumption

## Questions To Ask

- are the SLIs meaningful?
- are the SLOs realistic?
- are review cadences followed?
- are breaches visible to leadership?

## Evidence To Collect

- SLO documents
- SLI definitions
- review notes
- breach records
